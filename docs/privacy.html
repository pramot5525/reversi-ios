Privacy Policy

Revermoji  ·  Developed by Pramot

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how the Revermoji mobile application ("App", "we", "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our app. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

By downloading or using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

The App collects the following categories of data:

Data stored locally on your device

  • Game progress (total wins, earned coins)
  • Unlocked emoji pieces and customization choices
  • App settings and preferences

This data is stored only on your device using a local database and is never transmitted to our servers.

Data collected by third-party services

  • Apple Game Center — player identifier, leaderboard scores (total wins)
  • Google AdMob — advertising identifiers and ad interaction data (if ads are enabled)

How We Use Information

The data collected is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Saving your game progress and unlocked content between sessions
  • Displaying leaderboard rankings via Apple Game Center
  • Showing relevant advertisements through Google AdMob (free version only)
  • Processing your purchase of the "Remove Ads" upgrade via Apple In-App Purchase

We do not use your data for profiling, marketing, or any purpose beyond operating the App.

Third-Party Services

Google AdMob Advertising

The free version of the App displays ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve personalized or contextual ads. You can learn more and review Google's privacy practices at:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

Purchasing the "Remove Ads" in-app purchase will disable all advertising and stop AdMob data collection within the App.

Apple Game Center Leaderboards

If you are signed in to Game Center, the App submits your total win count to Apple's leaderboard service. Game Center is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy:

https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

Apple In-App Purchases Payments

All purchases are processed securely by Apple. We do not receive or store your payment information. Apple's payment handling is governed by their Privacy Policy above.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to any third party. Data is only shared with the third-party services described above (Google AdMob, Apple) as a necessary part of delivering their services within the App.

Data Retention & Deletion

All locally stored data (game progress, unlocked items, settings) is retained on your device until you uninstall the App. Uninstalling the App will permanently delete all locally stored data.

For data held by Apple Game Center, you may manage or delete it through your Apple ID account settings. For data held by Google AdMob, please refer to Google's data deletion options at myaccount.google.com.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us using the details below and we will take steps to remove that information.

Note: Google AdMob is configured to serve non-personalized ads in contexts where users may be under 13. If you have concerns, the "Remove Ads" purchase will disable all ads entirely.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of personalized advertising (via your device's privacy settings)
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email address below. You may also limit ad tracking on your device via Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising on iOS.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
